# Journal Articles (2026-04-13)

## Key Articles

**[Interpretability and implicit model semantics in biomedicine and deep learning](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-026-01177-0)** - *Nature Machine Intelligence*

This article examines the critical intersection of model interpretability and semantic understanding in biomedical AI applications. The research highlights how implicit model semantics—the unstated assumptions and learned representations within deep learning systems—can significantly impact both model performance and safety in high-stakes medical contexts. For AI safety, this work underscores the importance of developing robust interpretability frameworks that go beyond surface-level explanations to uncover the deeper semantic structures that guide model decision-making, particularly in domains where misalignment could have life-threatening consequences.
