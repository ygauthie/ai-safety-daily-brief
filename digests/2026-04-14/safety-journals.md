# Journal Articles (2026-04-14)

## Key Articles

**[Interpretability and implicit model semantics in biomedicine and deep learning](https://www.nature.com/articles/s42256-026-01177-0)** - *Nature Machine Intelligence*

This article explores the critical intersection of model interpretability and implicit semantic understanding in biomedical AI applications. The work addresses how deep learning models develop internal representations that may not align with human-interpretable concepts, particularly in high-stakes domains like healthcare where model decisions directly impact patient outcomes. The findings have significant implications for AI safety, as they highlight the challenges of ensuring reliable model behavior when implicit semantics diverge from intended use cases, emphasizing the need for robust interpretability methods in safety-critical applications.
